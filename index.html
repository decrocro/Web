How to prioritize revision

  1. Memorize definitions: project, project management, project life cycle, WBS, RACI, budgeting.

Revision Strategy

Exam Tips & Common Mistakes

High chance topics

  • Project life cycle
  • Team development stages
  • Business case vs project charter
  • Stakeholder Power/Interest Grid
  • WBS and RACI
  • Forward/backward pass
  • Critical path and slack
  • EV, AC, CV, CPI

Common mistakes

  • Confusing Responsible and Accountable in RACI.
  • Thinking highest spending means highest progress.
  • Forgetting Monitoring and Controlling happens during Execution.
  • Confusing Actual Cost with Earned Value.
  • Forgetting CPI < 1.0 means cost overrun.

How to answer subjective questions

  • Define the term first.
  • Give 2–4 key points.
  • Add a simple IT/cybersecurity example.
  • Use formulas where needed.
  • End with why it matters to project success.
